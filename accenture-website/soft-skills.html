🚀 Our Soft Skills Journey

🎤 1. How to Present

In our Workshop “How to present” we learned the best tips and tricks for presenting as well as how to use our body language, voice and confidence to our advantage. Afterwards we were able to test our new skills by spontaneously presenting with Karopop.

📝 2. How to Write a PowerPoint Presentation

After learning about how to present, we moved on to building a presentation by working in different phases:

  • 🗂️ Plan
  • 🧱 Structure
  • 🔧 Build
  • 🚀 Execute

We also discovered different types of presentations:

  • ℹ️ Informative - Facts, not emotional
  • 📚 Instructional - Direct instructions to explain a concept
  • ❤️ Arousing - Emotional to raise awareness of a problem/situation
  • 💡 Persuasive - Convincing by using arguments and emotion

📋 3. Project Management

  • ❓ What is PM? - Structuring processes, setting project scope, goals, identifying stakeholders and risks.
  • ✅ Top Tips - KISS (Keep it simple, short), communication is key.
  • 👥 Stakeholders - Categorized by influence and interest, included in communication accordingly.
  • ⚠️ Risks - Identify, Quantify, Prioritize, Plan Response, Assign Responsibility.

🌍 4. Self- & Cultural Awareness

Self-awareness is crucial for teamwork. Everyone must show empathy, collaborate, and self-reflect. There are 4 different social styles:

  • 😃 Expressive
  • 🤝 Caring
  • 🧐 Analyzing
  • 💪 Pushing

Cultural differences are important in global teams. Developing Self & Cultural Awareness helps us understand our coworkers better.

👑 5. Leadership and Communication Concepts

At Accenture, leadership is often lateral. During our training, we explored how a flat company structure fosters collaboration and leadership at all levels. For communicating efficiently, we leaerned about following steps to structure meetings and calls:

  1. 💡 inform
  2. 📉 analyze
  3. ↕ define
  4. 🎯 evaluate target
  5. 🔎 find solutions
  6. 🚀 realize
  7. ✅ check
  8. 🗯 feedback
